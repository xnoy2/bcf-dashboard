# CEO Dashboard — Product Demo Script
**Target length:** 6–7 minutes · **URL:** https://bcf-dashboard-production.up.railway.app
**Prep:** press the 🔄 refresh button ~2 min before recording · F11 full screen · log out first so the demo starts at the login.

---

## 0:00 — Opening (login page)
**Do:** Load the login page; let the splash animation play.
> "This is the new CEO Dashboard — a single, secure executive portal that brings every one of our business systems into one place: GoHighLevel, the BCF and BGR client portals, the Staff portal, Cloudflare, and GoDaddy. Live data, one login."

**Do:** Sign in.

## 0:40 — CEO Overview
**Do:** Pause on the overview. Hover the four KPI tiles.
> "The moment you land, you get the executive picture: total open pipeline value across all three businesses, this week's leads with the week-over-week change, active garden-room builds, and live staff numbers — every figure pulled directly from the source systems, refreshed automatically every five minutes. You can see exactly how fresh the data is, right here." *(point to "Data as of …")*

**Do:** Point to the Renewals card and the Security card.
> "It also watches the things that quietly go wrong — domain renewals coming due, and the security health of our whole web estate — with alerts when something needs attention."

## 1:30 — Everything is clickable
**Do:** Click a slice in Lead Sources → arrive on Sales pre-filtered.
> "Nothing here is a static picture. Click a lead source… and you drill straight into the actual leads behind that number."

## 1:50 — Sales Pipeline (the showpiece)
**Do:** On Sales — click "Meta Ads" in the donut; watch the trend redraw + Lead Explorer filter. Click a spike on the trend line. Type a name in search.
> "This is live cross-filtering. Pick a source — the trend redraws for just that source, and the Lead Explorer lists every individual lead: name, stage, value, date. Click a day on the trend — see exactly who came in that day. Over thirteen hundred leads, explorable in real time."

**Do:** Open the Pipeline-by-Stage selector, pick one pipeline (e.g. "BGR · Project Progress").
> "Every sales pipeline in the company, stage by stage."

## 2:50 — The account switcher
**Do:** Top-right: switch to "Bespoke Garden Rooms". Navigate to Marketing, then Finance — point out the selection sticks.
> "Want one business at a time? Switch accounts, and the whole dashboard follows you — Sales, Marketing, Finance — until you switch back."

**Do:** On Finance, point at Cash In.
> "Finance shows actual money collected through our payment system — not estimates."

## 3:30 — Delivery: Operations & Client Projects
**Do:** Switch back to All Accounts. Open Operations, then Client Projects. Click a slice in Builds-by-Status; flip the Stage Funnel selector to "BCF orders"; click a bar.
> "Delivery is where promises become installs. Every garden-room build with its live stage and progress, every climbing-frame order — pulled from the same portals the teams actually work in. Click any chart and the tables filter instantly. This progress bar? It's the same number the installation team sees, updated as they tick off stages."

## 4:20 — Staff
**Do:** Open Staff. Scroll roster, then the Job Schedule (point at vans/projects). Click a role slice.
> "The whole team — roles, hire dates, contracted hours — plus the live job schedule: who's assigned, which van, which project."

## 4:50 — Work Reports + export
**Do:** Open Reports. Click "In Progress" card. Click **Export CSV** and show the downloaded file.
> "One unified work report across all three systems — and any view you filter, you can export to Excel in one click. That's the weekly ops report, done."

## 5:20 — Oversight: IT Security & Renewals
**Do:** Open IT Security; scroll the domains table, then Web Apps. Then Renewals; click the red slice of the Renewal Health donut.
> "Forty-six domains monitored — HTTPS, email security grades, DNSSEC, certificate expiry — plus every web app and subdomain we run, health-checked. And renewals: anything expiring soon is flagged here, badged on the menu, and emailed to us automatically every morning at eight."

## 6:00 — The finish
**Do:** Toggle dark mode 🌙. Optionally drag the window narrow to show mobile layout. Click 🔄.
> "Dark mode for late nights, fully responsive on a phone, and a refresh button when you want this second's numbers. It runs in the cloud, updates itself around the clock, and there's nothing to maintain. One login — the entire group, live."

**Do:** End on the CEO Overview, full screen.

---

### Recording tips
- Speak ~10% slower than feels natural; pause 1s after each click so viewers register the change.
- If you fumble, pause 3 seconds and redo the sentence — easy to cut in Clipchamp.
- Mouse moves: slow, deliberate, point-then-click.
